The Positive Effect of Your Presence

Introduction

This paper seeks to find the positive effects of Valerie to the researcher. This research was made in preparation for Valentine's Day. The researcher first met her on August 29, 2023. Throughout the six months that have passed since then, various phenomena has occured to the researcher in terms of serendipitous or unexpected feelings. This seeks to find the trends in which these effects occur and how exactly it manifests in the heart of the researcher. This study aims to investigate the feelings exhibited by the researcher in terms of:

  • happiness;
  • shyness;
  • nervousness

Methodology

  1. Write a letter of confession to her.
  2. At the end of the letter, place the link of this study.
  3. mangulba ug maayo.

Results and Discussion

Figure 1. Levels of happiness in relation to presence

In Figure 1, the graph shows that her presence is directly proportional to the amount of happiness in a given day. It is noted that this effect was first observed during the senior high school Quiz Bee held on September, wherein the researcher felt embarrassment or shyness after seeing Valerie. Furthermore, this phenomena is also observed when she is absent from class.

Figure 2. Rate of shyness after time elapsed

In the initial months of this school year, the researcher observed that feelings of shyness were present when talking to Valerie in person. Despite this, the researcher was still able to hold conversations through messages without any major problems. It is noted that the researcher, on some occassions, cringed at what he said through text.

This effect has lessened in the following months. Whether it is due to the increase in the researcher's confidence or through other reasons is unknown. However, it can be said that the researcher heard the phrase "take the risk" maybe one too many times.
