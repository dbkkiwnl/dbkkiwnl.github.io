Matter and Its Properties

As a general starting point, chemistry is the study of matter and its properties, the changes that matter undergoes, and the energy associated with those changes.

Matter is anything that occupies space and has mass. It is composed of tiny particles called atoms.

States of Matter

Each state of matter is defined by how the sample fills a container.

  • A solid has a fixed shape that does not conform to its container. It is not defined by rigidity and hardness.
  • A liquid has a varying shape that does conform to its container, only to the extent of its volume; liquids have an upper surface.
  • A gas also has a varying that conforms to its container, filling the entire container.
