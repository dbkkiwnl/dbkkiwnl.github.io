Intermolecular Forces

There are four types of intermolecular forces:

  • hydrogen bonding
  • dispersion forces
  • dipole-dipole attractions
  • ion-dipole attractions

Hydrogen Bonding

A hydrogen bond is a special type of intermolecular attraction between hydrogen and other more electronegative atoms (fluorine, oxygen, nitrogen).

Some examples include hydrogen fluoride (HF), ammonia (NH3), and hydrogen sulfide (H2S).
